Composição

You will need: Clean jars with lids Distilled water Glycerin Plastic or ceramic animals, trees and/or figurines Tinsel glitter Epoxy Sand paper Step by step: Clean and dry the jar Scuff up the underside of the lid with sandpaper Atach the figurins using a bit of epoxy and let it set completely Fill the jar with distilled water and add a drop of glycerin to it Add the tinsel glitter to the jar Screw the lid on firmly and give it a shake
